About Amazon Restaurant

Welcome to Amazon Restaurant, a culinary gem in the heart of Kakamega, where we celebrate the rich flavors of Kenyan cuisine. Our journey began with a passion for sharing the authentic tastes of our heritage with the community.

At Amazon, we are committed to providing an alcohol-free dining experience, ensuring that everyone can enjoy our delicious food in a comfortable and welcoming atmosphere. Our menu features a variety of traditional dishes, prepared with fresh, locally sourced ingredients, and a touch of modern flair.

Our team is made up of dedicated individuals who are passionate about food and hospitality. From our experienced chefs to our friendly serving staff, we work together to create a memorable dining experience for each and every guest. We believe in creating a warm and inviting space where families and friends can gather to enjoy good food and good company.

We are more than just a restaurant; we are a part of the Kakamega community. We strive to support local farmers and businesses, and we are committed to sustainable practices that respect our environment.

Thank you for choosing Amazon Restaurant. We look forward to serving you and sharing our love for Kenyan cuisine.
